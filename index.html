Recommended Order

Your Learning Path

Follow this order to build knowledge progressively. Each module prepares you for the next.

01

Step 1 — Python Fundamentals

Begin with Python's built-in data structures. Understanding Lists, Dicts, and Strings forms the foundation of all data work.

02

Step 2 — SQL for Database Querying

Learn to query and transform data directly from databases. Indispensable for data pipelines and BI.

03

Step 3 — Excel for Business Analytics

Bridge the gap between code and business users. Learn advanced functions to analyze data in spreadsheets.

04

Step 4 — NumPy for Numerical Computing

Learn arrays and vectorized operations. NumPy is the backbone of scientific Python and powers Pandas under the hood.

05

Step 5 — Matplotlib for Data Visualization

Master data visualization techniques, charting, graphing, and styling configuration to bring your data to life.

06

Step 6 — Pandas for Data Manipulation

Apply your NumPy knowledge to real tabular data. Master DataFrames, cleaning techniques, and aggregation.
